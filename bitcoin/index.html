Statistic

Collateral Reserves - Total amount of Meridian Tokens currently held as Collateral.

Bitcoin Loans Granted - Total amount of Bitcoins loaned out to borrowers.

Window Open Until - Deadline for Bitcoin loan applications.

Balance

Balance window - Both your Bitcoin and Meridian balances are displayed in the Balance Window.

After accepting your loan repayment terms, the window will switch to display both your Collateral on Hold and Debt balances.

Collateral on Hold - The amount of Meridian that you have pledge as collateral to secure your loan – which we will return once you have completed your repayments.

Debt - The amount of Bitcoins that you are required to repay.

Loan window

Move the slider marker to select your desired loan amount.

Based on your desired loan amount, you will automatically be presented with specified collateral requirements.

After clicking the submit button, you are sent to the Repayment Terms window.

Repayment Terms - Your payments are dived into four parts, each of them must be completed on time.

To receive your loan, you must agree with this repayment terms.

After clicking the accept button, it switches to a Repayment facility.

 to 

This means that your loan has been granted.

To make a repayment, click the Repay button and your repayment will be automatically deducted from your Bitcoin balance.

The corresponding block is deactivated.

After full repayment of the loan, you will receive bonuses and be provided with favourable loan terms in the future.

To create a new loan, click the new loan button.

Credit meter

The credit meter displays your overall value as a borrower.

It contains the following levels:

POOR

FAIR

GOOD

EXCELLENT

Your ability to adhere to your repayment terms will cause your credit rating to change either positively or negatively.

Scenarios

You have the ability to see how your credit rating is effected based on the fulfilment or non-fulfilment of your repayment terms.

See what happens when you consistently complete your repayments on time by clicking the Positive Scenario button.

See what happens when you fail to make repayments on time by clicking the Negative Scenario button.

For each of the scenarios, you will be provided with an explanation on how your value as a borrower has changed.

Your credit meter will move to represent your standing as a borrower.

In a negative scenario, you can still submit a late repayment (click the Repay button) this will create a slight improvement on your performance.

Also the conditions on your future loans will be more workable.

Frequently asked questions

What is Meridian?
Meridian is a digital asset that is designed to be used in tandem with Bitcoin. Meridian tokens [MDN] can be used as collateral to secure Bitcoin loans from MDNHub.io - via our hub, you are able to earn bonuses and rewards for completing loan repayments.
Who is eligible to receive a loan?
Collateral and repayment requirements will vary based on your repayment history. Users that are able to meet these requirements are eligible to receive a loan.
How can I buy some Meridian?
Meridian tokens [MDN] can be purchased during our presale on February 7th 2017. After the presale, MDN will become tradeable on all alternative currency exchanges.
What is the procedure for applying for a loan?
You can apply for a Bitcoin loan at MDNHub.io and receive instant approval if you meet eligibility requirements.
Are there any consequences for non-repayment?
Overall, due to the bonuses and rewards that are available, more financial benefit is derived from maintaining and building a solid history of loan repayments. Borrowers who fail to repay their loans forfeit their collateral and are banned from the service.
What safety measures are in place to prevent abuse?
Due to Meridians’ ability to appreciate in value, it is perfectly moulded to perform its role as digital collateral. Should a borrower fail to make repayments, funds will be recovered via value appreciation of the forfeited collateral.
How many times am I able to receive a loan?
Each completed loan repayment boosts your value as a borrower, and provides you with access to higher value loans. However, you can only receive one loan at any one time.
Are loans paid out in Meridian (MDN) or in Bitcoin?
All loans are paid out in Bitcoin, provided that the required amount of Meridian is pledged as collateral.
Can I use any currency as collateral?
The only accepted form of collateral is Meridian [MDN]
Can third parties issue loans through the Meridian service?
We are not asking, encouraging or soliciting outside lenders to participate in the Meridian project. The responsibility of furnishing bitcoin loans rests solely on the Meridian team.
